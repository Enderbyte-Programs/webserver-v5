Signing up for 2024-05-25 bottle drive
How to use:
  1. Choose a route below
  2. If you are the only person doing this route, click "Submit and Reserve". If not, click "Submit" and wait for your friends to sign up before Reserving.
  3. Get the bag of flyers for your route form the band room.
  4. Deliver the flyers by May 19, 2024
  5. Return the bag and map to the band room
  6. On this website, click "Complete" on the route you did

What made Napoleon such a good general?

  • He had a photographic memory, meaning he could generate and read perfect copies of everything he saw.
  • He was also a military genius, and could meticulously plan battles down to a small scale.
  • He was extremely ambitious and would do anything to secure more power for himself and France.
  • He was very charismatic and was able to increase morale in his soldiers through motivational speeches.

Napoleon also used many military strategies during battle to get the victory:

  • Napoleon never used orthodox methods of attack.
  • He prided himself on being lightning-fast to catch enemies off guard.
  • Rather than face enemies head on, he would sneak up behind them.
  • He would use a figure-8 motion to divide and conquer an opposing army.

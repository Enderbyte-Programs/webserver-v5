Napoleon's Love Life

Reasons

  • Napoleon had some nonstandard reasons to start his search for love.
  • He wanted someone to teach him upper-class French norms.
  • He also wanted to connect with the French Nobility Network and secure more popularity.

Marriage 1 (1796 - 1810)

  • For that reason, he married Josephine, a widowed high-society Parisian lady.

A portrait of Empress Josephine
  • However, they had some love troubles.
  • Josephine was cheating on him.
  • She begged for another chance, which Napoleon granted.
  • A few years later, Napoleon divorced her, this time because of her failure to produce a son.

Marriage 2 (1810 - 1821)

  • Napoleon then married Marie Louise, an Austrian Duchess.
  • She did give birth to a son, which Napoleon named Napoleon II.
  • Marie Louise remained with Napoleon until his death.

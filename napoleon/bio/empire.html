EMPEROR Napoleon

  • Due to his constant success, Napoleon grew even more egotistical.
  • He did something that rendered him a massive hypocrite:
    • He did away with the consul system.
    • He declared France an empire and crowned himself Emperor.
    • He had fought for revolutionary ideals, and was now becoming the very thing he sought to destroy.
    • In a move of symbolic overruling, he placed the crown upon his own head, signifying his Absolute Power
  • English Historian Lord Acton said Absolute Power Corrupts Absolutely. This was definitely happening to Napoleon.
